EPC Certification

Who needs
a EPC certification

EPC certification is a legal requirement if your building, selling or renting a property. It aims to encourage property owners or landlords to be more energy efficient. An EPC gives a property an energy efficiency rating from A (most efficient) to G (least efficient) and is valid for 10 years. Our EPC certification starts from £50 (typical 3 bedroom house) and goes up depending on extra rooms.
